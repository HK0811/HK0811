Functions of the Discord Bot

You may access this page anytime by sending the help command to the bot.

"prefix"avatar "tag a random person"

The bot will send you the avatar of the person you tag.

"prefix"choose "some selections. No limit of selections"

The bot will choose one selection randomly

"prefix"delete "number of message to delete"

The bot will delete messages.

"prefix"help

The bot will send the link of this page.

"prefix"ping

The bot will send the latency between the bot and Discord server.

"prefix"qr "content"

The bot will generate a QR Code and sent it to you.

"prefix"say "message"

The bot will delete your command and repeat the message

"prefix"status

The bot will send you the status of the bot server including usage of CPU, CPU temperature, etc.
